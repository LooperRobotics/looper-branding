## 🌟 Brand Manifesto: Who We Are
We do not compromise with general-purpose vision solutions. **LooperRobotics is the pioneer of fully autonomous spatial intelligence cameras designed explicitly for "Physical AI".** To enable robots to truly master complex physical environments, we have deeply integrated the following four core pillars into the LooperRobotics brand, establishing a new perception benchmark for the Embodied AI industry:

* 🤖 **Born for Embodied AI**: Stripped of the historical baggage of traditional cameras, our architecture is built from the ground up purely for robotic spatial interaction, obstacle avoidance, and physical exploration.
* 📐 **Ultra-Wide Vertical FOV**: An industry-first maximum 188° ultra-wide lens combined with an enhanced vertical layout. We eliminate blind spots, reliably extracting reference points from ceilings to floors, even in dense crowds.
* 🚀 **The 24g Standard**: **A 24g accelerometer range is our newly defined industry standard.** It effortlessly handles high-dynamic movements—like parkour and jumping—for legged robots, ensuring output precision under extreme mechanical stress.
* ⚡ **Built-in vSLAM Pioneer**: We are among the very few fully autonomous spatial cameras on the market featuring "on-board compute." We deliver high-precision pose estimation directly at the edge, creating a closed-loop system for perception and mapping.

## 🚀 Core Product Matrix: Full-Stack Spatial Perception

Our product lineup consists of our flagship perception hardware and its powerful companion software, designed to solve the three major bottlenecks of Physical AI deployment: *Seeing clearly, navigating reliably, and deploying easily.*

### 1. Insight9 Fully Autonomous Spatial Intelligence Camera
As the core hardware portal for LooperRobotics, the Insight9 pushes the boundaries of robotic perception through four technical leaps:
* **Edge Compute Closed-Loop**: Built-in 10TOPS NPU smart computing chip. SLAM algorithms and AI binocular ranging run entirely on the camera, drastically reducing the host machine's computational load.
* **Millisecond Real-Time Response**: Relying on an edge-computing architecture, the "perception-action" loop operates in milliseconds, granting robots rapid, instinctive obstacle-avoidance capabilities.
* **Industrial-Grade Robustness**: Beyond the 24g IMU high-dynamic support, it features advanced strong-light suppression to guarantee stable performance in extreme physical environments with severe bumps or complex lighting.

### 2. Powerful Companion Software: tinynav & roboSpatial
Designed as the dedicated companion software for the Insight9 camera, these tools bridge the gap between perception and execution:

* **tinynav High-Performance Navigation Algorithm Library**:
  * **Generative World Model**: Breaking through the limits of traditional geometric measurement, it generates unlimited high-fidelity synthetic data from minimal real-world scene data, massively boosting generalization.
  * **Lightweight Logic**: Stripped of bloated, hard-coded rules, the **core code is streamlined to roughly 3,000 lines**, eliminating the reliance on complex manual tuning. Combined with the Insight9 module, it outputs control signals directly at the edge for safe exploration in unknown environments.
  * 👉 **[Explore the tinynav Repository](https://github.com/UniflexAI/tinynav)**

* **roboSpatial Spatial Editing Toolchain**:
  * **"Building Block" Interaction**: Features a highly visual interface, allowing operations personnel to plan robotic behavior and paths as easily as snapping blocks together.
  * **Exclusive Knowledge Base**: A modular design that structurally stores and intelligently calls upon environmental data, reducing deployment cycles from "months" to "days."

## 🌟 品牌宣言：我们是谁？
我们不做通用视觉的妥协者。**LooperRobotics 是率先为“物理AI（Physical AI）”专门设计的全自主空间智能开创者。** 为了让机器人真正适应复杂的物理世界，我们将以下四大核心特质与 LooperRobotics 品牌深度绑定，致力于确立具身智能行业的感知新标准：

* 🤖 **专为具身设计 (Born for Embodied AI)**：摒弃传统相机的历史包袱，从软硬件底层架构开始，纯粹为机器人的物理空间交互、避障与探索而生。
* 📐 **全域纵向视场 (Ultra-Wide Vertical FOV)**：开创性的最大 188° 超广角镜头与纵向视场增强布局。哪怕在密集人群中，也能从头顶和脚下精准提取参照物，彻底消除感知盲区。
* 🚀 **24g 行业新标杆 (The 24g Standard)**：**24g 加速度量程，是我们重新定义的行业标准。** 轻松应对足式机器人跑酷、跳跃等高动态运动，确保极端工况下的输出精度。
* ⚡ **内置 vSLAM 领跑者 (Built-in vSLAM)**：目前市面上极少数能做到“自带算力”的全自主空间智能相机。端侧直接输出高精度位姿，实现感知与建图的内闭环。

## 🚀 核心产品矩阵：全栈空间感知方案

我们的产品线由核心感知硬件及其强大的配套软件构成，精准破解物理AI落地中“看得见、走得通、用得好”的三大痛点：

### 1. Insight9 全自主空间智能相机：重塑空间感知的数据入口
作为 LooperRobotics 的硬件旗舰，Insight9 凭借四大维度的技术跨越，拓宽了机器人的感知边界：
* **端侧算力内闭环**：内置 10TOPS NPU 智能计算芯片，在相机端即实现 SLAM 算法与 AI 双目测距协同，将主机的计算负载降至最低。
* **毫秒级实时响应**：依托边缘计算架构，将“感知-行动”闭环控制在毫秒级，赋予机器人快速、本能级的避障反应能力。
* **工业级环境鲁棒**：除了 24g IMU 的高动态支持，结合强光抑制技术，确保在颠簸、复杂光照等极限物理环境中的稳定表现。

### 2. 高效配套软件：TinyNav & RoboSpatial
作为 Insight9 的核心配套软件，它们共同打通了从感知到执行的最后一公里：

* **TinyNav 高性能导航算法库**：
  * **生成式世界模型 (Generative World Model)**：突破传统几何测量的局限，仅凭少量真实场景数据即可生成无限量高保真合成数据，极大提升机器人的泛化能力。
  * **轻量化仿生逻辑**：摒弃冗长的人工规则代码，**核心代码精简至 3000 行左右**，摆脱对复杂人工调参的依赖。结合 Insight9 模组，直接在端侧输出控制信号，支持未知环境下的安全探索与长时记忆管理。
  * 👉 **[点击访问 TinyNav 开源仓库](https://github.com/UniflexAI/tinynav)**

* **RoboSpatial 空间编辑工具链**：
  * **“积木式”交互**：采用高度可视化的界面，让运营人员也能像“搭积木”一样快速规划机器人的行为与路径。
  * **建立专属知识库**：通过模块化设计，实现对环境信息的结构化存储与智能调用，将应用落地的周期从“月”大幅缩短到“天”。
